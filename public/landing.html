About LastChipStanding

LastChipStanding was created to solve the common problems of home poker games - keeping track of bets, turns, and pot sizes. It's designed to be simple enough for casual players but comprehensive enough for serious games.

No more arguments about whose turn it is or how much is in the pot - LastChipStanding handles all that for you, so you can focus on the game.
